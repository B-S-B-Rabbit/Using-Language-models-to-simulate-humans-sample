    About Us

    Our Project

    This project was developed as a coursework by two computer science students: Andrew and Ilya. They decided to perform a task as a course work, which consisted in implementing fine-tuning of the neural network on specific data that could simulate human behavior and providing the opportunity to use this by creating web applications based on the Django framework.

    The goal of this project is to provide a platform for users to ask a question and get an answer from the neural network.

    Our Team

    Ilya and Andrew are both second-year computer science students at HSE University. They have been working together on various projects since their first year, and have developed a strong partnership based on their complementary skills and shared passion for programming.

    Andrew is responsible for the web-development of this project, including the HTML and CSS, Django models, views, and templates. He enjoys designing user interfaces and making them visually appealing and easy to use.

    Ilya is responsible for the fine-tuning part of this project including the collection of databases and using them to train neural network.
