About Us

Our Project

This project was developed as a coursework by two HSE students. It consists in implementing fine-tuning of the GPT2 neural network based on compressed opinions of real people about a question and allows the user to receive a forecast answering a question from a given pool upon request. The Django framework was used for the web shell of this project.

The goal of this project is to provide a platform for users to ask a question and get an answer from the neural network.

Our Team

Our names are Ilya and Andrey. We are studying in the second year of the Higher School of Economics University in the direction of "Software Development". We have already worked together on various projects within the university, but this is our first experience. In this project, we had a lot of difficulties getting a server to train a neural network, so the final product turned out not to be what it was intended, but we hope that it will still be able to meet the necessary requirements.

In this project, in addition to general work, Andrey was responsible for creating a web shell for coursework, mainly including HTML and CSS, Django, neural network selection and website hosting.

Ilya was mainly responsible for training neural networks, working with data sets, their selection and analysis. This included working with libraries such as tensorflow and pytorch.
